ABOUT

The Disciplinary Committee of the JUIT Youth Club has been entrusting the task of maintaining the discipline during the colleges. The members of this committee work with determination to make certain that no misconducts of any kind take place during any event. They ensure that no scuffles happen and the events go on smoothly without any hindrance.
