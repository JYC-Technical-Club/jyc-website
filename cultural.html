CULTURAL AND DANCE

CLUB

ABOUT

The Cultural and Dance Club aims at moulding the personality of students and exposes them to a wide range of cultural events throughout the year. The Club also aims at developing the creativity of the students by providing and managing a multitude of themes and decorations throughout the campus during the fests. The club also manages all the independent dance societies in the college.
