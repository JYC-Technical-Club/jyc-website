ABOUT JYC

JUIT Youth Club (JYC) is the hub of enthusiasm and growth, expanding students' horizons beyond academics into sports, literature, culture, and technology. It fosters self-expression, recognition, and integration while building teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership. By organizing events and extracurricular activities, JYC provides a refreshing break from academic pressures, instilling confidence and a sense of achievement in students.

How a student
becomes a member?

Membership Eligibility and Structure
  • General Body Members: Students in their 1st and 2nd year can join as general members.
  • Coordinators and Mentors: Students in their 3rd and 4th year act as coordinators and mentors, guiding events and activities.
Joining Process
  • Form Submission: Interested students must fill out a form
  • Interview: A brief interview follows to assess the suitability of the candidates.
  • Club Participation: Each student can join 2 clubs and 1 committee for holistic development.
Contributions and Support
  • Annual Contribution: Students contribute Rs 1200/- annually to support JYC activities.
  • Institutional Support: JUIT may occasionally provide additional support based on requirements.
