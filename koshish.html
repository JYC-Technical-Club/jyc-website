KOSHISH

CLUB

ABOUT

Koshish is a one of its kind club that strives on doing its maximum to provide equal opportunity of quality education for all. Its volunteers help in providing relatively underprivileged children from nearby villages the standard of education that they were provided with. The club focuses on all-round development of both the students it teaches and its members.

EVENTS

Colours of Giving
  • "Colours of Giving" was a donation drive organized to support underprivileged children by collecting stationery, clothes, and monetary contributions. The event aimed to spread joy and provide essential resources to those in need, showcasing the power of community kindness.
Educational Workshop
  • An Educational Workshop was held at Government Senior Secondary School, Domehar, focusing on fundamental mathematics for younger students and Vedic mathematics for older ones. This initiative aimed to enhance educational experiences and empower underprivileged children.
Walkathon
  • The Vigilance Awareness Week featured a community walkathon and a street play by local children, promoting vigilance and integrity. These activities raised awareness about ethical conduct and accountability, highlighting the impact of youth initiatives in fostering a culture of integrity.
